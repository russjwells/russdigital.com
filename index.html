rootless

Website Screenshots Link

From 2011 to 2013 I cofounded a ride-sharing startup called rootless. I contributed UX Design and Front-End Development and wore many other hats along the way. In Fall of 2013 rootless went through the Betaspring Accelerator Program. I postponed my final year of design school one year to work on my business full time. I was one of three employee's on site in Providence, Rhode Island. There I helped grow our fledgling ridesharing website to 1000+ users by creating partnerships with music festivals and events.

  • Client: Rootless, Inc.
  • Date: 2011 - 2013
  • Service: UI/UX, Interaction and Web Design
